Sriperumbudur

Sriperumbudur is a town in Kanchipuram district, Tamil Nadu, India, located southwest of Chennai [2, 3].

Historical Significance

  • Birthplace of the Vaishnava saint Sri Ramanuja [1, 3].
  • Site of the assassination of former Prime Minister Rajiv Gandhi in 1991 [1, 3].

Industrial Hub

  • Rapidly growing industrial hub, particularly for the automotive industry [2, 3, 6].
  • Major auto component manufacturers and global car manufacturers have established a presence in the region [6].
